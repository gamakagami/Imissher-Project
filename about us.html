About Us

What is AstraCares?

AstraCares is a Caretaker Service website founded by Albertus,Gabriel,and Raphael in 2015. We focuses on giving parents a convinient way for parents to take care of their children while they are busy. Astra cares is 100 percent trusted and have been reviewed by millions of people in Indonesia.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be the trusted partner in nurturing happy childhoods, where parents find reliable and caring babysitters effortlessly, fostering a community where children thrive in a safe and loving environment.

our mission is to connect families with experienced and compassionate babysitters, creating a seamless and secure platform for parents to find the perfect childcare solution. We are dedicated to providing peace of mind, fostering positive relationships, and ensuring the well-being and joy of every child under our care. Through innovation, transparency, and a commitment to excellence, we aim to redefine babysitting services, making quality childcare accessible for every family.

Our Location
