History of AstraCares

Our Story

2001

In the bustling tech hub of Jakarta City, in the early 2000s, a group of parents who were also savvy entrepreneurs found themselves facing a common challenge â€“ finding reliable and trustworthy babysitters for their children. Fueled by the desire to create a solution for their community, they embarked on a mission to launch a platform that would redefine childcare.

The Start

2005-2006

AstraCares was officially born in 2005, a brainchild of the tech-savvy parents who understood the power of the internet. The founders envisioned a platform that would connect parents with qualified and vetted babysitters in their local neighborhoods. They wanted to make the process of finding trustworthy childcare as easy as ordering a pizza online.

Building Trust with Verified Sitters

2008

To build trust, AstraCares implemented a rigorous screening process for babysitters. Background checks, references, and interviews became standard procedures to ensure that only the most qualified and reliable individuals joined the platform. Parents could now confidently leave their children in the care of AstraCares sitters.

Expanding to Mobile

2012

As smartphones became ubiquitous, AstraCares adapted to the changing times. In 2012, they launched a mobile app that allowed parents to browse and book sitters with just a few taps. The app also introduced features like real-time updates, allowing parents to receive photos and messages from their sitters during the babysitting sessions.

Nurturing Future Generations

2020

Recognizing the importance of community, AstraCares introduced a feature that allowed parents to leave reviews and ratings for babysitters. This fostered a sense of accountability and helped parents make more informed decisions. The AstraCares began to grow, with parents and sitters forming connections beyond mere transactions.

Where We are at Today

2023

With years of success behind it, AstraCares has become a household name, synonymous with reliable and quality childcare. The platform continues to innovate, incorporating new technologies like AI-driven matching algorithms and expanding its services to include tutoring and special needs care. AstraCares remains dedicated to its mission of making parenting a little bit easier by connecting families with the best childcare providers in their communities.
