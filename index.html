Welcome to AstraCares!
Astra Cares is designed to provide a range of services and information related to caregiving, support, and assistance for infants, toddlers and childrens. Astra Care is also offering a convenient and efficient way for childcare services such as nanny, babysitters and Governess that is chosen based on their specific needs and preferences.
Babysitter
Supervises your children when you are not around occasionally.
Nanny
Regular support for after-school, weekend and weekdays.
Governess
Provides support for your children's education in your household.

Caretakers

Our caretakers are professionally trained by professionals and they are trained to provide essential care to the elderly, disabled and those with chronic illnesses, offering support that goes beyond just medical needs.
